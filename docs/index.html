Academic Workshop · Universidad Carlos III de Madrid

Date: 25 February 2026
Time: 16:00 – 20:30
Location: Costas Goutis room (10.0.23), Department of Statistics, Getafe

About the Workshop

This workshop focuses on methodological and applied aspects of interpretability and fairness in machine learning models. It aims to foster academic discussion and knowledge exchange among researchers at different career stages, with a particular emphasis on responsible data-driven decision-making.
